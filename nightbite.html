What is Night Bite?

Night Bite is a desktop action strategy game.

Help Lin race through a bustling night market, swerve around obstacles, and whack away rival stall owners all while collecting ingredients for their stall before the market closes.

We created this game in our CS/INFO 3152 class as part of the Cornell Game Design Initiative. As a designer, I helped draw art assets and animations, and attended many, many meetings to discuss design choices, write design documents and specification, and work out gameplay strategy. As a composer, I wrote the theme and in-game music, and created unique sound effects to fit the game's mood.
