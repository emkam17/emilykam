What is rapStudy?

rapStudy is a Cornell start-up dedicated to education through music. The rapStudy team is building an app that students K-8 can use to help them review and retain school content. We want to make learning fun and engaging for kids, so our product takes what students are learning in the classroom and transforms it into catchy, memorable songs.

I joined rapStudy as a songwriter, but soon transitioned to working as a designer and researcher. I have worked on creating a unique, supplementary game feature that allows the classroom to review all together, and have also helped build our ever-growing UI component library. I designed features for our mobile app (coming soon!) and set the design foundations for teachers to browse school standards on the rapStudy app.
